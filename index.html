OneTime

Official Website

OneTime is a Chrome extension for importing Excel workbook time entries into Certinia PSA timecards.

Availability

OneTime is distributed through a private or unlisted Chrome Web Store listing for approved users.

Contact

For support or product questions, contact onetime.extension@gmail.com.
